Lizards

Here is some knowledge I would like to share about lizards, in no particular order





Here are some facts about lizards. I am not technically an expert but I certainly have a lot that I would like to share!

  1. Lizard skin is not like human skin!
    • Lizards and humans both shed their skin but lizards do it all at once. Humans shed their skin in little flakes that turn into dust.
    • Some lizards can change color. They do this to blend in to their surroundings. People skin changes color too, but only if they are choking on food or especially angry, and they certainly don't blend in!
    • Lizard skin is made up of tough scales like armor. If you have a lizard, don't squish it though! Their scales aren't THAT tough!
  2. Lizard diets depend on the species. Here are some things that lizards can eat
    • Geckos: flies, moths, crickets, worms
    • Marine iguanas: seaweed
    • Bearded dragons: mice, fruit, vegetables, bugs
    • Komodo dragons: wildebeast, monkeys, people
  3. Lizard tails
    • As a defence mechanism, lizards can drop their tails
    • Often times, the tail will wiggle to trick the predator.
    • The tail grows back but it's always shorter and uglier.
